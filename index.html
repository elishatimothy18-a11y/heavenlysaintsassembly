About the Church

A House Built on God's Word

Founded: 13 September 2017

Heavenly Saints Worldwide Assembly was raised by God to be a beacon of His Kingdom in Abuja and across the nations. We are a Spirit-filled, Word-driven church committed to building men and women in the image of Christ — equipped for dominion, exploits, and the expansion of God's Kingdom.

Our Vision
Building a generation all over the world with undying passion for Jesus and the expansion of God's Kingdom through Salvation, Dominion and Exploits.
Our Purpose
To make every member of this great commission an agent of change in their community, their nation, and the world.
Mission Statement
As we GO, we preach the truth in love and boldness all over the nations of the world.
